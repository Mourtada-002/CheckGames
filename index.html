How to Play

CheckGames Gameplay

Learn how to play CheckGames

Each player starts with 5 cards

The primary objective of the game is to outpace your opponents by being the first to discard all the cards in your hand. Each player starts with a set number of cards, and through strategic play and careful consideration of each move, they must work towards emptying their hand.

Players take turns drawing and discarding cards

Gameplay proceeds in turns, with each player drawing a card from the deck and then discarding one from their hand. This cycle continues, requiring players to adapt their strategies based on the evolving game state and the actions of their opponents.

The game continues until one player has no cards left
